# Data Sources — Plastics Monte Carlo Simulation

Channel parameters in `mc_simulation.py` trace to the sources listed here.
The paper (Plastics: Thermodynamic Degradation Floor) is available on SSRN and contains the full
reference list and §4 calibration narrative.

---

## Channel Sources

### `C1_health_microplastics`

Endocrine disruptors, microplastic ingestion, PFAS-coated packaging, cancer risk. $1.0-3.0T/yr. Sources: Lancet, Minderoo Foundation, WHO.

*Full citations: paper §4 (available SSRN).*

### `C2_climate_ghg`

GHG emissions from petrochemical production (1.8 Gt CO₂e/yr × SCC). Sources: CIEL, IEA, EPA SCC.

*Full citations: paper §4 (available SSRN).*

### `C3_ocean_ecosystem`

Marine debris, microplastic contamination, coral reef damage, fisheries impact. Sources: Beaumont et al. 2019, UNEP.

*Full citations: paper §4 (available SSRN).*

### `C4_waste_management`

Municipal waste management, cleanup, landfill, incineration costs. Sources: World Bank, ISWA.

*Full citations: paper §4 (available SSRN).*

### `C5_environmental_justice`

Global South open burning, waste colonialism, fenceline community health burden. Sources: IPEN, Basel Action Network.

*Full citations: paper §4 (available SSRN).*

### `C6_governance_capture`

Petrochemical treaty sabotage, recycling theater, manufactured consumer blame. Sources: CIEL, UNEA INC negotiations.

*Full citations: paper §4 (available SSRN).*

## Industry Revenue (Π)

The private payoff Π = $650.0B/yr is global annual industry revenue.
Source: paper §2 and §4. See paper §DA-1 (Decision Audit Field 7) for full
revenue source documentation.
